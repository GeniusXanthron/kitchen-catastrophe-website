Story

You wanted to bake something, but suddenly, some animals broke into your house and stole your ingredients! So, you chase after them and complete mini games to get your ingredients back.

How to Play

Basic Instructions

  • Click to proceed to the next scene.
  • Space to retry after you lose a game.
  • Other instructions will be given at the start of a level.

Levels

Shoot the Birds

  • Move your cursor to aim and click to shoot the birds.
  • Don't let them get to the tree.
  • You can pass on to the next level after you shoot the bird with the flour.

Whack-a-Bunny

  • Click on the bunnies to get your eggs.
  • Avoid bunnies with the bomb.
  • You can pass on to the next level once you collect all of your eggs.

Platformer Chase

  • Use your arrow keys to move.
    ← to go left, → to go right, ↑ to jump.
  • You can move on to the next level after you catch the dog.

Ant Maze

  • Use your arrow keys to move.
    ← to go left, → to go right, ↑ to jump, ↓ to go down
  • Finish the two mazes in a minute to move on to the next level.

Remember and Bake!

  • Memorise all the ingredients in 10 seconds!
  • Enter how much of each ingredient you need.
  • You can move on after you get all correct.
